I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Purdue University under the supervision of Prof. Walid Aref. I am interested in database systems, query processing and data indexing, specifically for spatial data. Currently, my research focus is on efficient secondary-key indexing techniques for update-intensive workloads in database systems. I am also working as a full-stack developer in Rosen Center for Advanced Computing at Purdue University and collaborating with researchers in various fields.
